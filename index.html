Description

A rare American Arts & Crafts/Tudor Revival home that blends original character with thoughtful updates. Sunlit rooms and graceful proportions are suited to comfortable everyday living and entertaining. Convenient in-town/small town location amid beautiful pastoral landscapes close to farm markets, wineries/cideries, fine world-class dining, and the scenic Blue Ridge Mountains—yet less than 30 minutes to the large towns of Warrenton, Culpeper, and Front Royal, and less than 1 hour to Dulles International Airport in Northern Virginia and Charlottesville Airport. A quiet, connected oasis in a busy world!
